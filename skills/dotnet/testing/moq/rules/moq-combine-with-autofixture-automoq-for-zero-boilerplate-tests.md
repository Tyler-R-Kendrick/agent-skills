## Combine with AutoFixture AutoMoq for zero-boilerplate tests

Combine with AutoFixture AutoMoq for zero-boilerplate tests: use `AutoMoqCustomization` to auto-create all mocks and inject them into the SUT, reducing constructor changes from breaking every test.
