## Use `IReadOnlyList<T>`, `IReadOnlyDictionary<K,V>`, and...

Use `IReadOnlyList<T>`, `IReadOnlyDictionary<K,V>`, and `ImmutableList<T>` to prevent mutation of collections passed between functions.
