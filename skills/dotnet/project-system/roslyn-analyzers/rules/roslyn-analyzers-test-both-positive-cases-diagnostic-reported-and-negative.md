## Test both positive cases (diagnostic reported) and negative cases (no diagnostic) for every rule

Test both positive cases (diagnostic reported) and negative cases (no diagnostic) for every rule: to prevent false positives, which erode developer trust and lead to teams disabling the analyzer entirely.
