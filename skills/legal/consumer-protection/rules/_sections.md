# Consumer Protection Rules

Best practices and rules for Consumer Protection.

## Rules

| # | Rule | Impact | File |
|---|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Have legal counsel review all consumer-facing terms, pricing practices, and user interface flows. | HIGH | [`consumer-protection-have-legal-counsel-review-all-consumer-facing-terms-pricing.md`](consumer-protection-have-legal-counsel-review-all-consumer-facing-terms-pricing.md) |
| 2 | Design cancellation flows to be as simple as sign-up flows. | HIGH | [`consumer-protection-design-cancellation-flows-to-be-as-simple-as-sign-up-flows.md`](consumer-protection-design-cancellation-flows-to-be-as-simple-as-sign-up-flows.md) |
| 3 | Disclose all material terms before the consumer commits. | HIGH | [`consumer-protection-disclose-all-material-terms-before-the-consumer-commits.md`](consumer-protection-disclose-all-material-terms-before-the-consumer-commits.md) |
| 4 | Avoid dark patterns in all user interfaces. | HIGH | [`consumer-protection-avoid-dark-patterns-in-all-user-interfaces.md`](consumer-protection-avoid-dark-patterns-in-all-user-interfaces.md) |
| 5 | Implement age-appropriate design for products accessible to children. | HIGH | [`consumer-protection-implement-age-appropriate-design-for-products-accessible-to.md`](consumer-protection-implement-age-appropriate-design-for-products-accessible-to.md) |
| 6 | Use clickwrap consent for all terms of service. | HIGH | [`consumer-protection-use-clickwrap-consent-for-all-terms-of-service.md`](consumer-protection-use-clickwrap-consent-for-all-terms-of-service.md) |
| 7 | Send clear confirmation and renewal notices. | MEDIUM | [`consumer-protection-send-clear-confirmation-and-renewal-notices.md`](consumer-protection-send-clear-confirmation-and-renewal-notices.md) |
| 8 | Monitor enforcement trends and regulatory guidance. | HIGH | [`consumer-protection-monitor-enforcement-trends-and-regulatory-guidance.md`](consumer-protection-monitor-enforcement-trends-and-regulatory-guidance.md) |
